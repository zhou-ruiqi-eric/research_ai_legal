Y Combinator (Fall 2025 batch)

example: if you owns a small multifamily portfolio: 3 apartment buildings with 45 units total
 - She pays vendors manually (Home Depot for repairs, plumbers, utility companies, etc.) from different bank accounts.
- She keeps large **reserve cash** sitting in low-interest accounts (earning almost nothing).
- She manually tracks security deposits for every tenant and worries about compliance rules that change by city.
- She spends hours reviewing bills and has no idea she’s missing money she could claim back (tax credits, utility rebates, vendor discounts).
