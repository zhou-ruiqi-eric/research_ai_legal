Mandatory security rules for handling card payments. Non-compliance is expensive and risky.

- Global security standard protecting credit/debit card data (cardholder data).
- Who: Any organization that stores, processes, or transmits cardholder data (merchants, service providers, processors).
- **Structure**: 12 Requirements under 6 control objectives (network security, data protection, access control, monitoring, testing, policies).
- **Enforcement**: Contractual requirement by card brands (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.) — not a law.
- **Validation**: Annual self-assessment or QSA audit + quarterly scans.
- **Penalties**: $5k–$100k per month + higher processing fees or loss of card acceptance rights.
