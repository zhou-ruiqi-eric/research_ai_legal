Test how well a company protects and manages customer data

- System and Organization Controls 2
- Type 1
	- Focus: Design and implementation of controls at a specific point in time ("as of" a date)
- Type 2
	- Focus: Design plus operational effectiveness of controls over a period of time (typically 3–12 months, often 6–12 for maturity)
- Tests include: Security + Availability + Processing Integrity + Confidentiality + Privacy
