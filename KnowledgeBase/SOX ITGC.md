Mandatory IT foundation for trustworthy financial reporting under U.S. law

- IT General Controls required under Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) Section 404 for internal controls over financial reporting (ICFR).
- **Applies to**: All U.S. public companies (and IPO-prep firms).
- **Purpose**: Ensure IT systems that generate financial data are secure, reliable, and accurate to prevent material misstatements.
- **Key Areas** (4 main domains):
  - Access Management (logical access, provisioning, reviews)
  - Change Management (approvals, testing, documentation)
  - IT Operations (backups, monitoring, incident response)
  - Program/System Development (secure build & acquisition)
- **Tested by**: Management + external auditors (PCAOB) annually.
- **Consequences**: Material weakness = reported in 10-K, stock impact, SEC penalties.
