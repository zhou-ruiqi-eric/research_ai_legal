Systematically manage information security risks (confidentiality, integrity, availability).

- International standard for Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- - **Core requirements** (Clauses 4-10, mandatory):
    - Context of organization
    - Leadership & commitment
    - Planning (risk assessment/treatment)
    - Support (resources, training)
    - Operation
    - Performance evaluation (audits, reviews)
    - Improvement (continual)
- **Controls**: 93 in Annex A, grouped in 4 themes:
    - Organizational (37)
    - People (8)
    - Physical (14)
    - Technological (34)
- **Approach**: Risk-based (you justify which controls apply).
- **Certification**: Yes — 3-year certificate after external audit + annual checks. Globally recognized.
- Vs [[SOC 2]]: Broader (whole company system + continual improvement) and certifiable. [[SOC 2]] is US-focused report on customer data controls.
