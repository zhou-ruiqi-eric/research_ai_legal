Certifiable framework for trustworthy, ethical, and risk-managed AI

- World’s first international standard for **Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS)** (ISO/IEC 42001:2023).
- **Goal**: Establish, implement, maintain & improve responsible AI governance & risk management across full AI lifecycle.
- Who: Any organization that develops, provides, or uses AI systems.
- **Core requirements** (Clauses 4-10): Context, leadership, planning (AI impact & risk assessment), support, operation, evaluation, improvement (PDCA structure).
- **Controls**: 38 AI-specific controls in Annex A (focus: data governance, transparency, explainability, bias, ethics, human oversight).
- **Certification**: Yes — 3-year certifiable (with surveillance audits); globally recognized.
